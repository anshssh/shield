The Challenge

Stopping the Rising Issue of Illegal Waste Dumping

Inefficient Manual Monitoring

Manual monitoring of prohibited waste zones is **inconsistent and expensive**, failing to provide the 24/7 coverage required to effectively curb municipal waste dumping in urban areas.

Lack of Actionable Evidence

Without reliable, time-stamped **video and GPS evidence**, enforcement becomes difficult, allowing offenders to evade accountability, which contributes to escalating dumping issues.

Our Workflow

Automated Complaint Generation in 4 Steps

1. Motion Trigger

A **PIR sensor** (Arduino Uno) detects human presence near a restricted dumping area and triggers the system.

2. Video Evidence

The **ESP32-CAM** records a 10-second clip and the **SIM7670 LTE** module attaches accurate GPS and time data.

3. Cloud Analysis

Video is uploaded to a **Flask server**, where the **YOLOv5 AI model** analyzes the clip for illegal dumping action.

4. Auto-Reporting

If confirmed, an automated, evidence-backed **complaint report** is generated and forwarded to authorities via Telegram Bot.
